# Privacy Policy - TradingCourseArena

**Last Updated:** January 15, 2026

## Introduction

TradingCourseArena ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## Data Collection

### Data Collected

The TradingCourseArena application collects the following data:

1. **Account Information**
   - Email address
   - Password (encrypted)
   - Username (if provided)

2. **Progress Data**
   - Scores and statistics
   - History of answered questions
   - Time spent on each question
   - Progress by category (mental math, probability, etc.)

3. **Usage Data**
   - Training sessions
   - User preferences
   - Performance data

### Data NOT Collected

We do **NOT** collect:
- Precise location information
- Contacts from your device
- Photos or multimedia files
- Payment data (the app is free)

## Use of Data

Your data is used only for:

1. **App Features**
   - Save your progress
   - Generate personalized statistics
   - Improve your user experience

2. **Service Improvement**
   - Analyze overall performance
   - Identify bugs and issues
   - Develop new features

3. **Communication**
   - Send important notifications (if enabled)
   - Respond to your support requests

## Data Storage

All data is stored securely on:
- **Supabase**: Secure cloud infrastructure with SSL/TLS encryption
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with secure authentication
- **Authentication**: Secure password management (bcrypt hashing)

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT share your personal data with third parties**, except:
- If required by law
- To protect our legal rights
- In case of merger or acquisition (with prior notification)

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:
- Data encryption in transit (HTTPS)
- Secure authentication
- Restricted data access
- Regular backups

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access your data**
   - View all your data in the app
   - Export your statistics

2. **Modify your data**
   - Update your profile
   - Change your preferences

3. **Delete your data**
   - Delete your account at any time
   - All your data will be permanently deleted

4. **Disable your account**
   - Temporarily suspend your account
   - Reactivate at any time

## Cookies and Similar Technologies

The app uses local storage technologies (AsyncStorage) to:
- Save your preferences
- Maintain your session
- Improve performance
